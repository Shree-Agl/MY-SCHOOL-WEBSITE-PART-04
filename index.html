Hostel Life

Sanskar International school, Bargarh offers quality education with finest accommodation and living facilities to students from Grades 3 to 12. We wish to create opportunities for students to achieve high standards not just academically but also in their life as a whole. While preserving the best in traditional Indian and International education and culture, we have opted for a student-centric, value based learning and education system with ample opportunities for individual growth & development. Our world class facilities with state-of-the-art campus, CBSE curriculum, finest AC hostel rooms accommodation with reading table where student can read up to 09:00 pm and board appearing student can study till 11:00 pm, committed, qualified and experienced teaching and non teaching staff, seek to place SIS, Bargarh as one of the most admired, respected and renowned School. Our aim is to ensure that every student at our School is groomed to 'Realise his\her Potential' and be the best in every facet of life.
